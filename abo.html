About Our Academy

Welcome to Natya Kala Kendra a premier institution dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Indian classical dance.

With a legacy of over 3+ years of dedicated training and artistic excellence, our academy has become a nurturing ground for passionate learners who wish to explore the beauty of classical dance forms.

We offer structured and professional training in
Bharatanatyam – the classical dance of grace, discipline, and expression
Mohiniyattam – the elegant and lyrical dance form of Kerala
Kuchipudi – the dynamic and expressive dance-drama tradition

Our training focuses on building a strong foundation in rhythm (tala), expression (bhava), posture, technique, and stage performance skills. Students are guided step-by-step from beginner level to advanced stage performances.

We also emphasize:
Discipline and dedication
Cultural understanding of classical art
Stage confidence and performance exposure
Individual attention and professional grooming

At Chilanka Dance Academy, we believe dance is not just an art form, but a way of life, devotion, and expression of culture. Our mission is to inspire every student to grow as a confident performer and preserve the timeless beauty of Indian classical dance.
