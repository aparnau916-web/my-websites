About Our Academy

Welcome to Chilanka Dance Academy, a premier institution dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Indian classical dance.

With a legacy of over 3+ years of dedicated training and artistic excellence, our academy has become a nurturing ground for passionate learners who wish to explore the beauty of classical dance forms.

We offer structured and professional training in Bharatanatyam, Mohiniyattam, and Kuchipudi.

Our training focuses on rhythm, expression, posture, technique, and stage performance skills.

We also emphasize discipline, cultural understanding, confidence, and individual attention.

At Chilanka Dance Academy, dance is not just an art form but a way of life and devotion.
